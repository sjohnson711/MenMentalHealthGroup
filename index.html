BEYOND THE ARMOR

A monthly men's group dedicated to growth, authenticity, and connection

About The Group

Beyond the Armor is a safe place for men to shed the weight of the world. We are needing to place ourselves around other men who are facing a similar burden and become free together so we can be those men that we need to be for OURSELVES and then our FAMILY.

What To Expect

90-minute guiding meeting that involves professionals along as peers to process the difficulties in manhood. We are open to suggestions on how the meetings can be conducted. A place of love, patience, and understanding.
